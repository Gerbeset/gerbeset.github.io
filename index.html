Biography

I then attended the University of South Dakota, double-majoring in Mathematics and Philosophy. Currently, I am pursuing a Ph.D. in Mathematics at Texas A&M University under Dr. Jean-Luc Guermond. My passion is solving problems with logic and reason, and I consider mathematics one of the best ways to do so.
